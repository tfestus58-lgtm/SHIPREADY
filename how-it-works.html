How Kreddlo Works

Whether you are offering a service or hiring one, every step is clear, protected and built to work from anywhere in the world.

1

Step 01

Create Your Account

Sign up with your email address and choose your role as a freelancer. Your account is created instantly with no credit card required and no upfront payment.

2

Step 02

Complete KYC Verification

Submit your government-issued ID through Didit, our secure identity verification partner. Verification typically completes within 15 minutes and is required before your profile goes live.

3

Step 03

Build Your Profile

Set your title, skills, hourly rate and a short bio that tells buyers exactly what you offer. A strong profile with a clear starting price attracts higher-quality project inquiries.

4

Step 04

Get Hired and Sign the Contract

When a buyer sends a project offer, review the full scope and price before accepting. Kreddlo generates the contract automatically and both parties sign digitally before any work begins.

5

Step 05

Deliver Your Work

Complete the project and submit your deliverables through the platform. The buyer receives a notification to review and approve, and the escrow clock starts once delivery is marked.

6

Step 06

Withdraw Your Earnings

Once escrow is released, your balance updates in real time. Withdraw to your crypto wallet at the live exchange rate or cash out directly to your local bank account, with no manual steps and no waiting period beyond standard processing times.

Sell Products and Digital Downloads

Skip the contract entirely. List a product once and sell it again and again.

1

Step 01

Create a Product

Set a price, describe your offer and choose how it is delivered.

2

Step 02

Share Your Link

Every product gets a unique shareable link you can post anywhere.

3

Step 03

Get Paid Instantly

Buyers pay and receive delivery automatically. No back and forth required.

1

Step 01

Find a Verified Freelancer

Browse profiles in the marketplace and filter by skill, category or price. Every freelancer listed has passed Didit KYC verification, so you know exactly who you are working with before you reach out.

2

Step 02

Agree on Scope and Sign the Contract

Send a project offer with your requirements, deadline and agreed price. Kreddlo generates a binding digital contract and both parties sign before any payment is made or work begins.

3

Step 03

Pay Securely into Escrow

Pay the project amount in any of 300-plus supported cryptocurrencies. Funds are held in escrow and are never released to the freelancer until you approve the completed work.

4

Step 04

Review the Delivered Work

When the freelancer submits, you receive a notification to review the deliverables. You have a clear window to approve, request revisions or open a dispute if the work does not match the agreed scope.

5

Step 05

Approve and the Freelancer Gets Paid

Click approve and the escrow releases automatically. The freelancer receives their payment instantly and you receive a final contract PDF and receipt for your records.

Step 01

Step 02

Step 03

Why Kreddlo is Different

Built specifically for the markets that every other platform left behind.

Every Freelancer is Verified

No anonymous profiles. Every freelancer on Kreddlo has passed government-issued ID verification through Didit before their profile is published. Buyers always know exactly who they are hiring.

Powered by Didit KYC

Your Money is Always Protected

Payment goes into escrow the moment a project starts and stays there until the buyer approves the work. No freelancer can touch the funds and no buyer can pull them back without a dispute process.

Non-custodial escrow

Disputes are Resolved Fairly

When something goes wrong, both sides submit evidence and a trained Kreddlo admin reviews the full case. Rulings are based on the signed contract and the evidence, never on seniority or account age.

Human-reviewed decisions

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I sell digital products without doing custom work?

Yes. Create a product in your dashboard, set a price and share your product link anywhere including social media, newsletters or your own website. Buyers pay and receive instant automatic delivery. No contract or escrow required for product sales.

Does Kreddlo take a cut of product sales?

Yes the same platform fee applies to product sales as to service projects. You always see the exact amount you will receive before publishing your product.
