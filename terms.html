Our Services

Kreddlo provides a verified global freelance operating platform that connects KYC-verified freelancers with buyers from around the world. Specifically, Kreddlo provides:

  • A verified professional directory allowing buyers to find, evaluate, and engage identity-verified freelancers
  • Digital contract creation and signing tools to formalise service agreements between freelancers and buyers
  • Escrow payment infrastructure that holds buyer funds securely until work is delivered and approved
  • Cryptocurrency withdrawal tools allowing freelancers to receive earnings to their personal crypto wallets
  • Dispute resolution services to mediate disagreements between parties when they cannot reach resolution independently

Kreddlo is a platform facilitator, not a party to contracts between freelancers and buyers. We do not guarantee the quality of work delivered or the timely payment of funds beyond our escrow obligations.

Escrow

Kreddlo operates a milestone-based escrow system to protect both parties in every transaction.

  • Once a contract is signed by both parties, the buyer is required to fund escrow before the freelancer begins work. Kreddlo will not allow the freelancer to begin until funds are confirmed.
  • Funds held in escrow are controlled by Kreddlo and cannot be accessed by either party until a release condition is met.
  • Escrow is released to the freelancer's available balance when the buyer clicks Approve Delivery, confirming the work meets the agreed requirements.
  • If the buyer does not approve or dispute the delivery within 7 days of the freelancer marking work as delivered, escrow is automatically released to the freelancer.
  • In the event of a dispute, escrow is frozen pending Kreddlo's investigation and ruling. The funds are held until a final decision is made.
  • Kreddlo does not hold escrow funds in a personal bank account. Funds are processed through NOWPayments' regulated payment infrastructure.

Disputes

When a buyer and freelancer cannot resolve a disagreement independently, either party may raise a formal dispute through the platform.

  • Disputes must be raised within 7 days of the delivery being marked as complete. Disputes raised after this window may be declined.
  • Both parties will be asked to submit evidence including messages, files, screenshots, contract terms, and any other relevant documentation.
  • The Kreddlo dispute team will review all evidence and reach a decision within 3 to 5 business days of receiving complete submissions from both parties.
  • The Kreddlo team's decision is final and binding. By using the platform, you agree to accept this decision as the resolution of the dispute.
  • In cases of clear fraud or bad faith participation in the dispute process, the offending account may be suspended or permanently banned.
  • Kreddlo may rule for a full refund, partial refund, full release to the freelancer, or a split depending on the evidence.

Raising a dispute in bad faith, submitting fabricated evidence, or using the dispute system to harass the other party will result in immediate account suspension.
