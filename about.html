Our Story

The global economy locks out
the people who power it.

Every day, a designer in Lagos finishes a brand identity that a client in London loves. A developer in Accra ships clean code that a startup in Berlin calls production-ready. A writer in Karachi delivers copy that moves products for an agency in New York. The work is world class. The payment rails are not.

Stripe does not operate in Nigeria. PayPal withdrawals are blocked in Ghana and Pakistan. Wise restricts bank transfers across much of East Africa. These are not edge cases. They are the normal reality for tens of millions of skilled professionals whose only crime is where they were born. Kreddlo was built to change that, permanently.

The Kreddlo Mission

What We Stand For

Three principles.
Every decision runs through them.

These are not values written for a website. They are the constraints that every product and policy decision is measured against.

Value 01

Trust

Trust is not assumed on Kreddlo. It is built. Every freelancer profile is backed by government-issued ID verification before it goes live. Every payment sits in escrow until the work is approved. Every contract is signed by both parties and stored. When trust has a structure, neither side has to guess.

KYC verification on every account

Value 02

Transparency

You see the platform fee before you confirm any transaction. You see exactly what reaches your wallet and when. Dispute rulings come with written explanations. Fee structures are published, not buried. There are no hidden charges, no surprise deductions and no rates that change quietly after you sign up.

Full fee breakdown on every payment

Value 03

Access

The ability to earn from your skills should not depend on which country issued your passport. Kreddlo is built specifically for markets that traditional platforms have written off. Crypto-based payments mean no correspondent banking required. USDT payouts reach any wallet, anywhere, without a middleman deciding whether your country is worth serving.

Payments work in 80+ countries

Not another freelance marketplace.

Platforms like Fiverr and Upwork were built for markets where payment infrastructure already works. Kreddlo was built for everywhere else.
